## Message 2 (Threaded Reply)

Here's a rundown of everything you can do:

*Leaderboard* — Live standings with rank changes, filterable by champion pick, and "Who's Still Alive" insights
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app

*Bracket View* — Full interactive bracket with pick splits, team logos, and per-game pick counts. Filter by any bracket to see their picks highlighted across every game
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/picks?view=results&rview=bracket

*Heatmap* — See how the group picked every game at a glance. Click any matchup to see exactly who picked what
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/picks?view=results&rview=heatmap

*Simulator* — "Path to Victory" mode lets you fill in remaining games and see how standings shake out. Pin any bracket to see their best-case scenario
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/simulator

*Win Probability* — Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 iterations) showing each bracket's odds of finishing 1st, top 3, or top 10
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/probability

*Head-to-Head* — Compare any two brackets side by side to see where they agree and differ
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/head-to-head

*Awards* — Contrarian picks, most popular picks, chalk vs. chaos archetypes, and more
https://march-madness-dashboard-six.vercel.app/awards

:bulb: Pro tip: Select multiple brackets using the compare checkboxes on any page, then jump to head-to-head to compare them directly.
